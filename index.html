The Firm

The firm was created due to our passion for football but specially to offer a new concept related to football agents, who are too often not well seen. We, as footballers, have had bad experiences with our agents as well. These have had a significant negative impact on our careers.

What we Focus

Our mission

B.S.M want to be focused on acting well for the well-being of our clients. This comes before our personal interests, to avoid making the same mistakes that have killed our dreams in the past.

How we Work

Our work is also our greatest passion

Our way of working is based on being constantly present at football grounds and pitches. We want to meet the players as this step is pivotal to understand their needs and offer them a tailored service and make the most of our time and roles. We also built a strong network that brought us talent scouts in different countries such as Italy (whole country) Canada, Brazil, Cameron and Uganda, other than other contacts in Europe

About Clients

Who are our clients

Among our clients there are players who play in some of the best youth teams in Italy. Some of them, decided to be managed by B.S.M when they were already playing for professional teams. Others, instead, were playing in local teams and have been brought to the next level thanks to our passion and dedication. We scouted them, helped them to understand what their ambitions were and supported when hard time arose by being available for them whenever they needed us.

Ethics

Who we are looking for

At B.S.M are strict about players behaviour and attitude. To become part of our family, players, managers and partners must show respect, manners and politeness towards rivals, agents, teams and to the role they have. A player who does not succeed in showing these value simply cannot be part of B.S.M, no matter who he is or who he may become.
